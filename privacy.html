Think Then Play Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Think Then Play (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Think Then Play website and mobile application (collectively, the “Service”).

1) Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Name or display name (if provided)
  • Email address (for account access, support, or follow-up)
  • Saved training screens, drills, preferences, and settings you create
  • User submissions (if you choose to submit a drill), such as a drill title and a video link

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and basic device identifiers (non-precise)
  • Basic usage data (features used, screens viewed, session length)
  • Crash logs, performance diagnostics, and error reports

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as medical records, precise location, or biometric identifiers. Please do not submit sensitive information through the Service.

2) How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • Create and manage accounts and authentication
  • Save and load your training screens, drills, and preferences
  • Review and respond to drill submissions and follow up if needed
  • Understand how features are used to improve performance and usability
  • Fix bugs, improve stability, and enhance app performance
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with you about the Service

3) Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers to help operate and improve the Service. These may include:

  • Firebase Authentication (to sign users in and manage accounts)
  • Firebase Firestore (to store app data such as saved screens and drill submissions)
  • Crash reporting and diagnostics (to identify bugs and improve performance)

These providers may process limited data on our behalf and are subject to their own privacy and security obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

4) Sharing of Information

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: with vendors who help us operate the Service (as described above)
  • Legal and safety: if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the Service
  • Business changes: if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets (information may be transferred as part of that transaction)

5) User Submissions and Public Sharing

If you submit a drill (for example, a video link) you understand that we may review it and, if approved, feature or repost it within the app or on social channels, as described in our Terms and Conditions.

Please do not submit links or content that include personal or sensitive information you do not want shared.

6) Children’s Privacy

Think Then Play may be used by athletes under the age of 18 with parental or guardian supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent from a parent or legal guardian.

If you believe a child has provided personal information without consent, please contact us and we will take appropriate action, which may include deleting the information.

7) Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information using administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8) Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Access or update your account information (where available in the app)
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Requests can be made by contacting us at the email address below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

9) Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the Service, for legitimate operational purposes (such as security and fraud prevention), or to meet legal requirements. For example, we may retain certain records to comply with law, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.

10) International Users

If you access the Service from outside Canada, your information may be processed and stored in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in Canada, the United States, or other locations where our service providers operate.

11) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent version. Continued use of the Service means you accept the updated policy.

12) Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:
Email: hello@thinkthenplay.com
