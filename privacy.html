Think Then Play Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Think Then Play (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Think Then Play website and mobile application (collectively, the “Service”).

1) Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Name or display name (if provided)
  • Email address (for account access, support, or updates)
  • Saved training screens or preferences you create

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Basic usage data (features used, screens viewed, session length)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as medical records, precise location, or biometric identifiers.

2) How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and improve the Service
  • Save and load your training screens and preferences
  • Understand how features are used to improve performance and usability
  • Fix bugs, improve stability, and enhance app performance
  • Respond to support requests

3) Analytics and Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics, crash reporting, or cloud storage providers) to operate and improve the Service.

These providers may process limited data on our behalf, subject to their own privacy and security obligations. We do not sell your personal data.

4) Children’s Privacy

Think Then Play may be used by athletes under the age of 18 with parental or guardian supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

If you believe a child has provided personal information without consent, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

5) Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information using administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6) Your Choices

You may:

  • Access or update your account information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Requests can be made by contacting us at the email address below.

7) Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the Service or meet legal and operational requirements.

8) International Users

If you access the Service from outside Canada, your information may be processed and stored in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

9) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent version. Continued use of the Service means you accept the updated policy.

10) Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:
Email: hello@thinkthenplay.com
