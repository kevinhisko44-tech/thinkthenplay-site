Think Then Play is designed to train the mental side of performance — the skills that show up in every sport:

  • Faster reaction time
  • Quicker decision-making
  • Improved focus under pressure
  • Better anticipation and pattern recognition

The app doesn’t replace physical training — it enhances it. By training how your brain processes visual information and reacts to cues, you improve how quickly and confidently your body responds during real game moments.

Because the training is sport-agnostic, athletes use it across hockey, basketball, football, soccer, combat sports, fitness, and rehabilitation — adapting the same cognitive skills to different environments.
